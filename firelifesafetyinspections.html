Fire & Life Safety Inspections

Protecting Lives, Preserving Compliance

Ensure your facility's fire protection systems are functioning as intended with expert inspections from TAB Services Company. Our certified Colorado-based technicians specialize in fire and smoke damper inspections and fire door inspections, helping your facility maintain compliance with NFPA standards, AHJs, and local life safety codes — including requirements from the City and County of Denver and The Joint Commission.

We provide comprehensive testing, documentation, and compliance verification to meet regulatory requirements and support ongoing facility safety. Should we identify any deficiencies, our team works closely with facility managers to coordinate and perform necessary repairs.

Why Regular Inspections Matter:

  • Ensure functionality of critical life safety components
  • Prevent the spread of smoke and fire through HVAC systems
  • Comply with NFPA and AHJ-mandated inspection intervals
  • Maintain accurate records for audits and inspections

With TAB Services, you get fast, reliable service and peace of mind knowing your building's fire protection systems are in expert hands.

01

Fire & Smoke Damper Inspections and Repairs

Fire and smoke dampers play a critical role in preventing the spread of fire and smoke through HVAC systems. In addition to our inspections, our team assists with fire damper repairs to ensure your facility remains in compliance and your building occupants are safe. The Joint Commission requires hospitals to perform fire damper inspections every 6 years and senior living facilities to perform fire damper inspections every four years.

Our inspection process includes:

  • Functional testing to verify proper operation
  • Comprehensive AHJ documentation
  • Repair and remediation assistance
  • Damper location identification
  • ID and date-tested stickers
02

Fire Door Inspections

Fire doors are essential barriers that slow the spread of fire and smoke, protecting occupants and property. The Joint Commission mandates that healthcare facilities conduct annual inspections of all fire door assemblies to ensure compliance with the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) standards.

Our fire door inspections include:

  • A 10-point inspection process for every fire rated door assembly in your facility
  • Verifying compliance with NFPA 80 and NFPA 101 standards
  • Detailed and easy to read reports for code compliance and facility managers

Protect lives, property, and ensure code compliance with TAB Services. Contact us today for expert fire and smoke damper inspections and fire door inspections!
