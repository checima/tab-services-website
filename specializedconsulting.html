Specialized Consulting Services

The TAB Services team is an extension of your team. With over 200 years of combined industry experience, we provide the expertise and support needed to keep your project running smoothly, efficiently, and successfully.

01

Design Review

We leverage our in-field balancing expertise to provide expert HVAC design review services.

Our team evaluates mechanical designs and equipment lists alongside a project's mechanical engineers and contractors to ensure things are planned properly from the start of a project.

  • Code & Standard Compliance – Verification of compliance with local building codes and commissioning requirements.
  • System Efficiency Analysis – Identifying opportunities for energy savings and performance optimization.
  • Air & Water Balancing Considerations – Ensuring proper distribution and control for occupant comfort.
  • Constructability & Maintainability Review – Addressing potential installation or maintenance challenges.
  • Coordination with Other Trades – Preventing conflicts with electrical, plumbing, and structural elements.

By addressing design flaws before construction begins, we help reduce costly rework, improve system longevity, and ensure smooth project execution. Trust us to deliver HVAC systems that perform as designed—efficiently, reliably, and compliantly.

02

Sourcing and Inventory Management

We provide expert sourcing and inventory management services through our trusted supplier network ensuring you have the right HVAC equipment, parts, and materials—exactly when you need them.

Whether you're managing new construction, system upgrades, or routine maintenance, our team streamlines the procurement process to reduce downtime, control costs, and keep your projects running smoothly.

  • Equipment & Parts Sourcing – Identifying and procuring high-quality HVAC components from our trusted supplier network.
  • Vendor & Supply Chain Management – Leveraging industry relationships to secure competitive pricing and reliable delivery.
  • Inventory Tracking & Optimization – Ensuring you have the right stock levels to avoid shortages and delays.
  • Emergency & Just-in-Time Procurement – Rapid response to urgent needs to minimize disruptions.
  • Customized Solutions – Tailored strategies to align with your project scope and operational requirements.

With TAB Services, you get a partner dedicated to efficiency, cost savings, and reliability in HVAC inventory management. Let us handle the logistics so you can focus on delivering top-tier service performance.

03

HVAC Commissioning

We provide comprehensive HVAC commissioning services to ensure installed systems operate as designed—efficiently, reliably, and in full compliance with industry standards. Our HVAC commissioning services engagements are best utilized by building owners/developers or general contractors. Our expert team verifies that all HVAC components are installed, tested, and functioning optimally, reducing energy waste, improving occupant comfort, and preventing costly post-installation issues.

  • New Construction Commissioning – Verifying system performance from design through installation and final testing.
  • Retro-Commissioning – Evaluating and optimizing existing HVAC systems to improve efficiency and reliability.
  • Functional Performance Testing – Ensuring all HVAC components operate according to design specifications.
  • Air & Water Balancing Verification – Confirming proper airflow and hydronic system performance.
  • System Documentation & Training – Providing detailed reports and staff training for ongoing system operation.

Our commissioning services give you the confidence in knowing that your HVAC systems are tested, optimized, and built for long-term performance. Whether for new construction or existing buildings, we help you achieve energy efficiency, regulatory compliance, and cost savings.
