Biological Safety Cabinet and Fume Hood Testing Services

TAB Services Company specializes in Biological Safety Cabinet (BSC) testing and certification to NSF/ANSI 49 standards and fume hood testing and certification to ASHRAE 110 standards. Ensuring the safety and performance of your laboratory containment equipment is critical for both personnel protection and compliance with industry standards.

For equipment that tests out of the acceptable parameters, our team will also assist with repairs.

With our team on your side, you can trust that your lab equipment is performing at peak safety and efficiency. Ensure compliance, protect personnel, and maintain a safe working environment—contact us today for expert BSC and fume hood certification services.

01

Biological Safety Cabinet (BSC) Testing & Certification

BSCs are essential for maintaining a sterile and contamination-free work environment. Our certified professionals perform comprehensive testing to verify:

  • Airflow Integrity – Ensuring proper airflow patterns and velocity
  • HEPA Filter Integrity – Leak testing to confirm effective filtration
  • Containment Performance – Verifying operator and product protection
  • Operational Safety – Compliance with NSF/ANSI 49 requirements
  • Repairs and Calibration – Fan speeds, motor issues, and HEPA filter repairs
02

Fume Hood Testing & Certification

Properly functioning fume hoods protect lab personnel from exposure to hazardous vapors and chemicals. We provide detailed testing based on ASHRAE 110 standards, including:

  • Face Velocity Testing – Ensuring adequate airflow for containment
  • Tracer Gas Containment Testing – Evaluating hood effectiveness in containing hazardous substances
  • Smoke Visualization Testing – Identifying airflow patterns and potential leaks
  • Performance Verification – Confirming compliance with safety standards
  • Repairs and Calibration – Fan speeds, motor issues, and HEPA filter repairs
