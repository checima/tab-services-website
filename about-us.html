01 — Who We Are

About Us

TAB Services Company is the largest and most experienced testing, adjusting and balancing firm in the Rocky Mountain region. Since 1981, we have been entrusted with keeping the most highly-sensitive indoor environments in the region operating at peak performance levels. Our air balancing work keeps your patients, employees, customers, and occupants safe and comfortable.

Our talent and experience separate us from our competitors. Our team has a combined 200+ years of technical balancing experience as a resource to provide you with project solutions. We have successfully completed over 7,000+ projects and resolved issues on the most complex mechanical systems in the Rocky Mountains. TAB Services technicians undergo a rigorous multi-year training to qualify as the lead on any project. We have the largest number of certified technicians in the Rocky Mountain region; all of our trained technician experts are certified with NEBB and/or ICB/TABB. Most of our competitors cannot say the same.

Contact us today and let us show you why the top facilities in the region trust us to keep their systems operating at peak performance levels.
